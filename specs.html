The Smart LoBo Training Kit is designed with the following specifications:

Specification Details
Microcontroller ESP32, ESP8266
Operating Voltage 5V-12V
Communication Protocols WiFi
Power Supply 9V 2A (Negative Center, Positive Outer)
Display 20x4 LCD with I2C
Buttons 3 push buttons(Enter, Option, Back)
Input Switches 5 SPDT Switch(HIGH(1), LOW(0))
Mode Switch 1 (Training Mode, IoT Mode)
Segment Display 1 inch 7-Segment Display Common Cathode
Connectors JST XH2.54 (10cm, 20cm)
Logic Gates ICs Not (7402), AND (7408), OR (7432), NAND (7400), NOR (7402), EX-OR (7486), EX-NOR (4077)
Binary Coded Decimal IC CD4511
Foldable Yes
Handle Yes
Lock 2 Latch
Magnetic Yes
